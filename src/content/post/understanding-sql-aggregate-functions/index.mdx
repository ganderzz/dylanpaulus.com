We gain insights into our products, applications, and customers by exploring our data. PostgreSQL exposes aggregate functions that give us the tools to transform and process our data to provide meaning.

In this article, we'll take a look at how to use SQL aggregate functions, the pitfalls, and how Timescale gives us advanced tooling to aggregate time-series data.
